 # Rigid/unplasticised PVC:
  - Gutters, drainpipes
  - Window frames
  - Cladding
  - Can be foamed

 # Plasticised PVC:
  - Hosepipes
  - Wire Insulation
  - Adhesive Film

 - Additives can improve heat and UV performance.
 - Do not laser cut: Produces Chlorine Gas
