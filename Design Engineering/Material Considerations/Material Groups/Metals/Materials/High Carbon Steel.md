# Makeup:
 - 0.6% to 1% [[Carbon]]
 - 0.3% to 0.9% [[Manganese]]
 
# Properties:
 - High resistance to wear.
 - Brittle.
 - Poor corrosion resistance.
 
 # Uses:
 - Hand and machine cutting tools that need to retain their sharp edges.
