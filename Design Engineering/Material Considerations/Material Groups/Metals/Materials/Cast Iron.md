Used a lot by the Victorians.

# Modern Uses:
  - Machine beds.
  - Engine Castings
  - Vice Bodies
  - Pans

# Properties:
  - Cost Effective
  - Heavy
  - Strong in Compression
  - Casts Well
  - Easily Machined.

Traditional Cast Iron has a very high [[Carbon]] content, which makes it very brittle. Nowadays, Cast Iron is more often cast [[Mild Steel]].
