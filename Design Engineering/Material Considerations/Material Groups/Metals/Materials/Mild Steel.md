# Makeup:
 - < 1.65% [[Manganese]]
 - 0.6% [[Silicon]]
 - 0.6% [[Copper]]

# Properties:
 - Tough
 - Ductile
 - Malleable
 - Good tensile strength
 - Easily welded.
 - Poor resistance to corrosion
 - Cannot be hardened except for case hardening.
 
- Used for General Purpose engineering and structural material.
