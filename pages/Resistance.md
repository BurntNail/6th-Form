- As the electrons flow through the metal, the electrons hitting the atoms make the resistance.
- $$V = IR$$
- Resistance depends on a number of variables:
	- Temperature
	- Material
	- Length
	- Thickness
- $$R = \frac{\rho L}{A}$$
	- |Symbol|Meaning|Units|
	  |--|--|--|
	  |R|Resistance|$\Omega$|
	  |$\rho$|Resistivity|$\Omega m$|
	  |A|Area|$m^2$|
	  |L|Length|$m$|
