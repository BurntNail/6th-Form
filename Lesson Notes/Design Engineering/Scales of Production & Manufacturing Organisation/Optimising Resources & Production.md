## [[Injection Moulding]]
 - Make the wall thickness consistent (around 2mm) and prefer ribs over thickening.
 - Provide generous fillet radii.
 - Avoid using blind holes.
 - 1$\pu{^\circ}$ draft angle min, no undercuts.
