Twisting forces are known as [[Torque]] and measured in [Nm].
Moments are static forces also measured in [Nm].

$$Moment = Force * Distance\ from\ Pivot$$

In equilibrium:
$$\sum{Clockwise\ Moments} = \sum{Anticlockwise\ Moment}$$
