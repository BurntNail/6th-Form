## Open Loop System
 - No feedback
 - Sometimes needs a datum
## Closed Loop System
 - Sensors feedback to microcontroller
 - Microcontroller reacts & adjusts output
 - **Must be a feedback loop** - bimetallic rice cookers & kettles **do not count**
