## How to Identify
 - Hollow shape with one hole.
 - Draft angles everywhere
 - Made from a thermoplastic
 - Possible marks from trimming of parison/flash

## Examples
 - Water bottle
 - Dish soap bottle
 - Shampoo bottle
 - Syringes
