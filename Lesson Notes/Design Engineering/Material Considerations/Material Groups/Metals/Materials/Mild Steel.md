# Makeup:
 - < 0.25% Carbon
 - < 1.65% [[Manganese]]
 - 0.6% [[Silicon]]
 - 0.6% [[Copper]]

# Properties:
 - Tough
 - [[Ductility|Ductile]]
 - [[Malleability|Malleable]]
 - Good [[Tensile Strength]]
 - Easily welded.
 - Poor [[Corrosion Resistance]]
 - Cannot be hardened except for case hardening.

# Uses:
- General Purpose engineering
- Structural material.
